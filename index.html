About This Academy

Eleven Academy is one of the largest online education portal that unifies learners, teachers and Parents on single interactive interface and provides environment conducive of learning to students. Role of Parent in educating children cannot be neglected as they monitors the learning of child at home. In modern educational system, “Educational triangle” comprises of Student/Learner, Teacher and Parent.
